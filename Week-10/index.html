About Us

Gennaro's is a casual, yet trendy establishment offering mouthwatering specialty pizza, thin crust tomato pies, and made to order Italian cuisine at affordable prices. We offer menu additions featuring only the finest, fresh, seasonal ingredients. Our family friendly atmosphere and intimate covered outside dining area is what sets us apart. We look forward to serving you!
